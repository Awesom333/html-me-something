Coding And What I Hope To Do With It...A Solemn Vow

This is a personal promise page to encourage continued progress

The possibilities of coding are near boundless. The tech industry has a plethora of moves to make in terms of innovation. I am someone with a significant amount of interest; that being the case coding can be a way to apply a multitude of those interest in a relevant fashion. Here I'd like to explore my previous fascination with poetry. I wrote a lot while I was in high school and regret, in some ways, stopping. I shall reiterate a promise to myself...

For the longest time I've sought to be useful The thought was so sublime Not succeeding made me rueful Now here I am years later Always feeling relativily old I'm unwilling to meet my maker Until I've used my starting gold With each skill I build The closer to whole I'll be I shall never yield Until I am free To the future self, I say now This is my solemn vow...

Believe it or not that was my first poem in over 12 years. I must say I'm quite proud of it. I cannot however forget what it means. Coding is yet another skill to complete a greater vision of myself. Something I think everyone should be able to have is self trust. Now I have a site in which I can visit in my low and high times. Thank you Launchcode!
