A Solemn Vow

This is a personal promise page to encourage continued progress and a better future

No matter how bad it is or how bad it gets you're going to make it. Believe that everyday nothing will stop you. This is your motivation page. The place where when you are looking down you can begin to look up! The world is your oyster. A personal poem awaits you below. Take your time here and gather yourself for the long journey ahead!

For the longest time
You've sought to be useful
The thought is so sublime
Not succeeding made you rueful

Now here you are years later
Always feeling relativily old
Unwilling to meet your maker
Until you've used your starting gold

With each skill you build
The closer to whole you'll be
Never shall you yield
Until you are free

To the future self, you say now
This is your solemn vow...

Believe in yourself and the future will be bearable. Dreams will be fulfilled, and the world will be a better place. Make no your vitamin and press forward. Shoot for the stars, beacuse even if you miss, you'll land amongst the stars. You're as limited as the universe. Come here for a boost anytime!
